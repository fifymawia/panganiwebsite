Athletics At Pangani Girls' High School

Pangani actively participates in music festivals where our choir team carried home a trophy the year 2017 and 2018 proceeding to the national level. Pangani has also been able to send out some of its students to participate in games each team bringing home lots of trophies and medals.

The main aims of the Athletics include the following:

  • To educate
  • To stimulate thinking
  • To Keep Students Fit
  • To entertain
  • To develop creativity
  • To Enhance Talents
  • To provide an avenue for socialization
  • To help students grow spiritually and morally upright
  • To help students develop and exploit gifts and talents
