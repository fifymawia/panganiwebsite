School Admission

How we do our Admissoins

Welcome to pangani girls high school. we do admissions for both form ones and continuing transfer students Admissions are approved by the principal and the deputy.

Download Admission Form

Admission Processes

  • Form1 Intake

    Kcpe Placements

    These are the class 8 leavers who passed Well in their KCPE and have been placed to pangani girls. The admissions are done in january where a reporting date for all the intakes is set. The reporting day the students must be accompanied by parents or a gurdian and should carry with them the following documents: Admission Letter
    Primary School Kcpe Results
    Bank slip of school fees
    and a fully filled admission form

    Continuing Transfer Intake

    Transefer Students

    Pangani girls' school is well known for its high discipline. The students are taught to uphold virtues such as integrity and hard work just as the school motto states. "Founded on truth". The discipline gained is key to success Therefore admission of a transfer student depents on why the student left the previous school and the history of the students perfomance.

    Form1 New Students

    Students looking for placement in Form1

    This are the students that were placed in other schools after passing their kcspe exams but prefer to school at pangani girls. their admission depends on their results and why they prefer pangani girls high school
