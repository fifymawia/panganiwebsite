Subjects and Exams

COMMON SUBJECTS

Compulsory To All Students

Mathematics, English and Kiswahili are compulsory to all students from Form 1 to Form 4.Even when the students are choosing subjects in form two, These three are mandatory and the school provides adequate materials for better perfomance

OPTIONAL SUBJECTS

Choice Subjects

These are the subjects that students have to choose the ones they are best in. For Humanities, they have to choose any Two between History,Geography and C.R.E . For Sciences they have to choose any two between Chemistry, Physics and Biology Or do the three. Then Between Computer Studies, Agriculture and Home Science They choose one.

PRACTICALS

Lab Subjects

Biology, physics and chemistry have practicals that must be done in the labs. we have all the thre labs well equipped and fully functional.Practicals are key to passing the sciences Both in local exams and national exams. We allow students to practice more oftenly for better results. The other Lab is the computer lab where computer students Interract with the computers one on one.

EXAMS

OPENING EXAMS

Every First week after opening the school there is an exam to freshen up the students minds for the beginning of the term. This exam counts on the End term exams

MIDTERM EXAMS

Every First week after midterm there is an exam to freshen up the students minds for the beginning of the second half of the term. This exam also counts on the End term exams

CLOSSING EXAMS

The closing exam is the main exam of the term and is combined with the other two done earlier in the term to get the average marks.

NATIONAL EXAMS

This is the Exam done by form 4's and it is a common exam done country wide by all school regardless of the level of the high school
