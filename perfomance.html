Perfomance and Legacy

Our Perfomance Over The Years

Pangani Girls High School is a girls national secondary school located along Juja road in Nairobi County. Pangani girls' school is well known for its high discipline. The students are taught to uphold virtues such as integrity and hard work just as the school motto states. "Founded on truth". The discipline gained is key to success even in the outside world as it opens up to job opportunities

Our Achievements

  • 2000-2017

    Results Ranked Top 1st

    Academics are the school core business and therefore we are proud to always produce the best performers in the country in the national exams.Talking from the last two years, in 2016: we were position 11 with 9.56 and in 2017: we were position 10 with 9.2, not to mention producing the best student NAOMI KIRIMI who took the school to another level.

    2011 - 2012

    Best Teachers

    In the competitions at the national level in Mombasa, we got the first runners up. This goes a long way to show that our teachers are some of the best there are. Our school was recently nominated for teacher of the year award, TOYA and principle of the year awards, POYA. In the competitions at the national level in Mombasa, we got the first runners up. This goes a long way to show that our teachers are some of the best there are.

    2004 - 2017

    Games

    Pangani actively participates in music festivals where our choir team carried home a trophy the year 2017 and 2018 proceeding to the national level. Pangani has also been able to send out some of its students to participate in games each team bringing home lots of trophies and medals.
