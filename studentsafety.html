Students' Safety

Pangani Girls High School is a girls national secondary school located along Juja road in Nairobi County. The school being located near the the road, we have full time gate security and the gate is always closed. Incase of strangers coming into the school, a thoruogh check is done and they have to produce their national ID and give a clear explanation of where they are going and who they are going to see.

The Foot Print/Flyover

The footptint allows safe movement of the stdents from the classes to the dorms since the two are located far apart and there is a major and busy road between them. Students are not allowed to use the gate while going to the dorms in any way.
The day schoolers are expected to sign in and out at the gate digitally to help monitor their movements in and out of the school.

Students relation with

Staff

The school ensures that the student-staff relationship is highly professional and they are monitored, incase of any doubts the school immediately takes action. The students are adviced to report any harrasments by staff and also take care of each other. The Matrons protect the students once they go to their domitories with the help of the watchmen at the dorm area.
